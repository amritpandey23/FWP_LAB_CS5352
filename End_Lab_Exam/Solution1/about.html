About Me

Hi, My name is Amrit! I am a programming and software development enthusiast. Fixing things and finding solutions to unsolved problems is my Ikigai. Internal working of Computers and their quirky functionality have always enticed me. I've always tried to develop my applications in the most unconventional way to produce as less legacy code as possible. I am a great fan of Engineering Practices, Style Guides, Linting, Refactoring and Tutorial based Documentations. I also like to write tutorials and simplify difficult-to-understand tools. In my coding experience so far the most important lesson I've learned is
"With a properly defined function, it becomes unecessary to know how it is done, knowing what is been done is sufficient" - Brian Kernighan.
